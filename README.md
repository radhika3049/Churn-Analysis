# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard** developed using **Power BI** to identify the factors contributing to customer attrition, uncover high-risk customer segments, and provide actionable recommendations to improve customer retention.

The dashboard follows a **business storytelling approach**, guiding stakeholders from understanding the overall churn situation to identifying root causes and prioritizing retention strategies through advanced analytics.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

The primary objectives of this project were to:

* Analyze customer churn behavior and patterns.
* Identify the key factors influencing customer attrition.
* Detect customer segments with the highest churn risk.
* Estimate the business impact of churn.
* Provide data-driven recommendations to support customer retention initiatives.

## 📂 Dashboard Structure

### 🏠 1. Welcome Page

Provides an executive-level overview of the customer base and overall churn performance.

#### Key Metrics:

* 📌 Total Customers
* 📌 Churned Customers
* 📌 Churn Rate (%)
* 📌 Average Monthly Charges
* 📌 Average Tenure (Months)
* 📌 Revenue at Risk

#### Visuals Included:

* Customer Churn Distribution
* Customer Distribution by Tenure Group
* Interactive Slicers for Data Exploration

---

### 📉 2. Churn Analysis

Investigates the reasons behind customer churn by analyzing customer behavior across multiple dimensions.

#### Visuals Included:

* Churn Rate Across Tenure Groups
* Customer Churn by Contract Type
* Impact of Tech Support on Customer Churn
* Customer Churn by Internet Service
* Customer Churn by Payment Method

#### Key Findings:

* Customers with **month-to-month contracts** exhibited the highest churn rates.
* Customers with **lower tenure** were more likely to leave.
* Lack of **technical support** significantly increased churn risk.
* **Fiber optic customers** showed relatively higher churn rates.
* Customers using **electronic check payments** demonstrated higher churn propensity.

---

### ⚠️ 3. Customer Risk Analysis

Leverages advanced Power BI capabilities to identify high-risk customers and uncover the root causes of churn.

#### Advanced Visuals Included:

* Top 5 High-Risk Customer Segments
* Key Influencers
* Decomposition Tree

#### Key Insights:

* Customers without **Online Security** were significantly more likely to churn.
* Absence of support-related services increased churn likelihood.
* Specific combinations of customer attributes contributed disproportionately to churn.
* High-risk segments were identified to support targeted retention strategies.

---

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

* 📌 Month-to-month contract customers represented the most vulnerable customer group.
* 📌 Early-stage customers showed a higher probability of churn.
* 📌 Support and security services played a critical role in customer retention.
* 📌 Payment behavior emerged as an important churn indicator.
* 📌 High-risk customer segments can be proactively targeted to reduce attrition.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following actions are recommended:

* Encourage customers to transition to long-term contracts.
* Promote support and security services to increase customer satisfaction.
* Develop proactive retention campaigns for high-risk segments.
* Focus onboarding and engagement efforts on newly acquired customers.
* Prioritize interventions for customer groups contributing most to churn.
