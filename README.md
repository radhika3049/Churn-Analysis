📊 Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard | Power BI
Project Overview

Developed an interactive Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard in Power BI to identify the key drivers of customer attrition, analyze churn patterns, and uncover high-risk customer segments. The dashboard follows a business storytelling approach, enabling stakeholders to move from understanding overall churn trends to identifying root causes and actionable retention strategies.

🎯 Objective

The primary objective of this project was to:

Analyze customer churn behavior.
Identify factors contributing to customer attrition.
Detect high-risk customer segments.
Provide data-driven recommendations to improve customer retention and reduce revenue loss.

🛠️ Tools & Technologies
Power BI
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
Power Query
Data Modeling
Interactive Visualizations

📌 Dashboard Structure

1. Welcome Page
Provides an executive overview of the customer base through key performance indicators and customer distribution insights.

Key Metrics:
Total Customers
Churned Customers
Churn Rate (%)
Average Monthly Charges
Average Tenure (Months)

2. Churn Analysis
Investigates the factors associated with customer churn through segmentation analysis.

Visuals Included:
Churn Rate Across Tenure Groups
Customer Churn by Contract Type
Impact of Tech Support on Customer Churn
Customer Churn by Internet Service
Customer Churn by Payment Method

3. Customer Risk Analysis
Leverages advanced Power BI capabilities to identify high-risk customers and uncover the root causes of churn.

Advanced Visuals Included:

Top 5 High-Risk Customer Segments
Key Influencers
Decomposition Tree

🔍 Key Insights
Customers with month-to-month contracts exhibited the highest churn rates.
Customers without online security and technical support were significantly more likely to churn.
Electronic check users showed a higher propensity to leave.
Customers with lower tenure were more vulnerable to churn.
High-risk customer segments were identified to support targeted retention initiatives.

💡 Business Recommendations
Encourage customers to transition to long-term contracts.
Promote support and security services to reduce churn risk.
Implement proactive retention campaigns for high-risk segments.
Focus engagement efforts on newly acquired customers during their early lifecycle.

📈 Outcome
This project demonstrates the ability to transform raw customer data into actionable business insights using Power BI. It combines executive reporting, diagnostic analysis, and advanced analytics to support data-driven decision-making and customer retention strategies.
